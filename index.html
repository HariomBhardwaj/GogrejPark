About us

Our story began as part of India’s Swadeshi movement - the original ‘Make in India’ movement.

Established in 1990, Godrej Properties is the first real estate company to have ISO certification. The company is currently developing landmark projects in 12 cities across India covering over 12.93 million square meters. Godrej Properties is known to bring innovation and excellence to the real estate industry.
